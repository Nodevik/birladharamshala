About Us

Nestled in the heart of Ayodhya, Birla Dharamshala is a serene retreat that blends comfort, cleanliness, and spiritual ambiance for pilgrims and visitors alike. Located just steps from key temples and holy sites, our dharamshala offers well-appointed rooms with modern amenities—from clean linens and ensuite bathrooms to vegetarian dining that honors local tradition.
